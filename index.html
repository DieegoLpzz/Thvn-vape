THVN Vape Review

Product: HispaCBD Skittles 95%

Overall Effects

Energizing, sativa-like cerebral high with light body relaxation. Mild couch-lock.

Onset Time

Takes around 20–30 minutes to feel full effects.

Duration

Decent duration for a vape. Doesn’t fade quickly.

Taste & Discretion

Tasty and subtle Skittles flavor. No smell left in the room. Very discreet.

Side Effects

Dry mouth and slight manual breathing. Can be harsh if overused quickly.

Hardware Quality

Feels cheaper than other devices. Harsh on multiple hits, but functional and battery performs well.

Rating

8 / 10

Best Use Case

Ideal for people who want a weed-like high discreetly—perfect for environments where traditional cannabis isn’t possible.
